2) Every VSCO girl NEEDS stickers on their Hydro Flask, right? Well instead of wasting tons of paper and plastic from store-bought stickers, you can make your own out of recycled paper. There are several DIY sticker-making videos on YouTube, and you can modify them by using recycled or scrap paper!
